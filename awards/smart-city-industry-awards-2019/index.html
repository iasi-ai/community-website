Smart City Industry Awards 2019

Organized by The Romanian Smart City Association, Smart City Industry Awards is the most appreciated smart city event by the leaders involved in the construction, consolidation and development of creative-intelligent communities in Romania. The main objective of the event is to encourage innovation and competitiveness in Romanian cities and to ensure efficient management for our communities.

At the 4th edition of the gala, which took place on December 11th 2019, IAȘI AI won the Smart Citizen / Civic Engagement category award with Iași Smart City initiative.

Read more in Ziarul de Iași newspaper or the official press release from Romanian Smart City Association.
