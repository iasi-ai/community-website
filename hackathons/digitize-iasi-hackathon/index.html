Digitize Iași Hackathon

On May 20th, 2023 IAȘI AI organized Digitize Iași Hackathon, first community hackathon, where participants thought about ways to digitize Iași, working on themes like: promoting and incentivizing public transportation using gamification, noise pollution and building an intelligent traffic light system, reducing intersection congestion.

Engineers, UX designers and product people worked together in deliverying better solutions for Iași. This was just a first step and we'll continue working together on trying to bring these ideas to the next level.
