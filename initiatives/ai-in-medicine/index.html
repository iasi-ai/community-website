AI in Medicine

Between November 2020 - January 2021, Alexandru Burlacu, Head of Department of Interventional Cardiology at the Institute of Cardiovascular Diseases Iași brought together several people from both academic and business and launched a new AI course for Medicine for students of "Grigore T. Popa" University of Medicine and Pharmacy Iași.

From IAȘI AI community, Gabriel Marchidan covered Present and future in AI, while Eugen Bușoiu covered NLP (natural language processing): theory and application and Virtual healthcare - Siri, Alexa, Google Health project ETHICS in AI & data issues, confidentiality, GDPR.
