AI as a growth accelerator

  • Thu, Apr 25th, 2024, 18:00 - 20:00
  • Visma (Palas Campus)

On April 25, we're meeting with Jan Strandbakke, Head of Development (CTO) at Tripletex Norway for a conversation about the transformative power of artificial intelligence as a catalyst for business growth and innovation. As AI is revolutionizing industries and reshaping customer experiences, we'll be exploring Jan's current and future perspectives.

Meet the speaker

Jan Strandbakke Head of Development (CTO) at Tripletex

Jan Strandbakke is an experienced leader and technologist, passionate about building high-performing teams and delivering exceptional software. With a great product and brilliant people, he has been able to scale the Product and Development department to over 130 talented individuals, improving team productivity, delivering up to 30 features a day with outstanding quality.
