Engineering Intelligence Through Agent Interaction

  • Wednesday, March 20th, 2024, 18:30 - 20:00
  • Fab Lab 3

Robotic process automation becomes agentic process automation in the realm of digital processes. Generative AI is expected to be the platform enabling the replacement of menial manual labor, especially in digital workflows. However, engineering solutions that couple simpler components to manifest higher intelligence may require both cross-domain expertise and novel social and paradigmal considerations.

Meet the speaker

Igor Ševo Head of AI at HTEC

Igor Ševo is a computer scientist, writer, and theorist with a Ph.D. in machine learning. His published scientific work is relevant to transfer learning, convolutional neural networks, and specialized algorithms and architectures. Currently, he is head of artificial intelligence at HTEC Group, but as an independent educator and a former Mensa member, he has authored interdisciplinary work across a range of diverse fields, including philosophy, mathematics, literature, and arts.
