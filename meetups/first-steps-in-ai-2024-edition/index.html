First steps in AI, 2024 edition

  • Friday, May 17th 2024, 12:00 - 14:00
  • Faculty of Computer Science

On May 17th, we'll be hosting the forth edition of First steps in AI, an event for all AI passionates that would like to study or continue studying artificial intelligence in one of the available academic programs in Iași. If you have questions about AI education, it's a great opportunity to directly interact with academic experts and gain clarity on program structures, adminission requirements, career prspects and more.

For this event, our guests from Faculty of Computer Science, Mathematics, Automatic Control and Computer Engineering and Faculty of Electronics, Telecommunications and Information Technology will present the academic programs and courses that cover specific #AI topics in the upcoming academic year.

The event will take place at Faculty of Computer Science, C309 amphitheater.
