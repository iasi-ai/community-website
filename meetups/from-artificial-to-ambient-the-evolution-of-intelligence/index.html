From Artificial to Ambient: The Evolution of Intelligence

  • Wednesday, November 15th 2023, 18:30 - 20:00
  • Fab Lab 1

George will explore the key principles and strategies for ensuring the ethical and reliable use of AI in this fast-evolving landscape. He will share new insights into how to develop AI products that not only harness the potential of ambient intelligence but also earn the confidence and trust of the AI community and society at large.

Join us in this exploration of the future of AI, as we bridge the gap from the artificial to the ambient and embark on a journey towards a more intelligent and trustworthy future.

Meet the speaker

George Serediuc Lead UX Designer at Cisco

George Serediuc is a Lead UX Designer at Cisco. He loves to build products that are purposeful and human-centered. Spending 12+ years across industries, he's helped several successful start-ups and some already established brands along the way, by designing various solutions from e-commerce and enterprise platforms, to Android, Windows, or iOS apps. George's number one mission in design is to craft beautiful digital products that are simple and easy to use.
