Towards Common-Sense Reasoning with Advanced NLP

  • Wednesday, November 13th 2019, 18:30 - 20:30/span>
  • Fortech

After a quick review of the history of the #NLP breakthroughs, we will discuss the most recent architectures which attempt to give #AI a deeper understanding of language and visual concepts.

More concrete, we will discuss variants of #LSTM-based and more recent attention-based models and how they fare on harder NLP benchmarks.

Our speaker is Alex Movila, software engineer at Fortech and passionate about deep learning.

Meet the speaker

Alex Movilă Senior Java developer at Fortech

Since 2001, Alex has worked as a Java developer for several companies (Iași and Netherlands) and in the recent years he became passionate about deep learning.
